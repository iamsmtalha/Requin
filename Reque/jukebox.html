Welcome to the pulsating heart of Reque.in —

The Jukebox section, where you're not just part of the night, you're shaping it. Here's how you can dive into the action and play maestro:

  1. Spot Your Spot: Enter any partnered venue, and our app seamlessly syncs you with the local Jukebox. Whether it's a bustling bar or a cozy café, you're in control.
  2. Pick Your Anthem: Browse through an exhaustive library of tunes, genres, and artists. From the chart-toppers to the obscure gems, find that perfect song that fits the moment.
  3. Send Your Request: With a simple tap, shoot your track straight to the DJ's playlist. Want to dedicate it to someone special? Add a personal message to give it that extra touch.
  4. Bid for the Beat: Is there a song that just can't wait? Place a bid! Outbid others to jump the queue and get your song played. It's fair, fun, and instantly gratifying.
  5. Enjoy the Moment: Watch the crowd groove to your tune. It's not just about listening to music; it's about creating an experience for everyone around you.
